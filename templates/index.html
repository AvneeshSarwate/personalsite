About Me

A Little About Me

I am a software engineer, musician, and music technologist currently living in New York City. My work focuses mainly on building novel systems for live electronic music performance using commodity musical hardware.

I offer a complete web solution to my clients. I can guide you through the early stages of project planning and research, help you with information architecture and user experience, design your website, and finally build the site for you.
